Arriving without dying.
Apr 16, 2017
1 minute read
  • bike
  • backpack
  • idea
  • motivation

How can I get somewhere I’ve never been, while not negatively impacting my safety as a cyclist?

I try to be a safe biker. I wear my helmet. I use lights. I don’t look at my phone. I never, ever wear headphones. I realize that biking, even in a city with as much bike infrastructure as Montreal, has some inherent dangers, so I do my best to make sure that I’m not limiting myself by blocking off what I’m hearing.

All this to say, it can be difficult to receive directions while I’m biking. At the end of last summer, I decided that I needed to do something for myself so that I could bike places that I hadn’t been to before, without being unsafe. Given my skill set and education, there seemed to be only one solution

Make my backpack smart, and figure out a way for it to give me haptic directions.
