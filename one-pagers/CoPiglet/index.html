Bike Commuting

Clean, green, zero-emission commuting. When you bike commute, the only thing you'll be emitting is sweat. If you're lucky, your office has a shower. If not, you're going to want to bring some baby-wipes or something. Maybe use the sink.

Carrying stuff while bike commuting

You're a mean, green, sweaty commuting machine. Bring a change of clothes. Bring a change of clothes in your sweet, sweet backpack. It's cool as heck, and you probably are too.

Taking your bike commute off the commute

You've mastered the post-commute sink shower, now take yourself off that regular commute. Bike somewhere new. Don't know where you're going? Have directions, but can't hear your phone in your sweet backpack? Don't worry, if you could buy a CoPiglet, and you did buy one, you could get directions from your backpack. Haptically.
